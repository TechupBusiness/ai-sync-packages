```markdown

{{PROBLEM_DESCRIPTION}}

{{USER_PERSONAS}}

## Platform

{{PLATFORM_TYPE}} — {{PLATFORM_DETAILS}}

## Key Differentiators

{{DIFFERENTIATORS}}

## Success Metrics

{{SUCCESS_METRICS}}

## Constraints

{{CONSTRAINTS}}
```
